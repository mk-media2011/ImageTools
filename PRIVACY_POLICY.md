# Privacy Policy for Image Tools

Last Updated: May 2026

The Image Tools browser extension is built to operate locally.

## 1. Data Collection and Tracking
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension does not use analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts. There are no background servers logging your usage.

## 2. Image Processing and Conversion
All image format conversions (such as converting to PNG, JPEG, PDF, or Base64) are processed locally on your device within the browser. Your images are never uploaded to a cloud server for conversion.

## 3. Reverse Image Search and Social Sharing
Image Tools provides shortcuts to third-party services:
* Search Engines: Google Lens, Bing, Yandex, etc.
* Social Sharing: WhatsApp, Reddit, Telegram, etc.

When you use a search or share feature, the extension passes the public URL of the selected image to the corresponding third-party website. We do not intermediate this connection. Once redirected, the privacy policies of those respective third-party platforms apply.

## 4. Permissions Used
The extension requests the following permissions to function:
* contextMenus: To add actions to the right-click menu.
* storage: To save UI preferences locally on your device.
* downloads: To save converted images to the file system.
* scripting and activeTab: To show visual feedback within the active page.
* offscreen: To process image data locally in the background.
